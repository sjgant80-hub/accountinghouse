The client-portfolio kernel

Every client is a sub-kernel.

AccountingHouse's kernel is a client-portfolio kernel. Each client is a separate entity block with its own accounting reference date, VAT scheme, chase cadence, fee arrangement, HMRC references. When AI drafts an email or return, it grounds in that specific client's sub-kernel.

The chaos-vs-on-top spectrum is encoded per client. Chaotic sole traders get warm nudges with cheerful language. On-top Ltd directors get formal reminders with next-step precision. The AI doesn't send both the same message.

No fabricated advice. The kernel is what stops the AI from inventing a scheme election or misstating a threshold. If a fact isn't in the kernel, the AI says it doesn't know and flags for you.
